Dr. Les Kicklighter, DMD

Dr. Les, a native of Reidsville, Georgia, moved to Statesboro in elementary school. In high school he achieved the rank of Eagle Scout and in 1993 he graduated as an honor graduate from Statesboro High School.

He graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Biology from Georgia Southern University in 1997. He graduated with his Doctorate of Dental Medicine from the Medical College of Georgia in 2001. He has been taking care of patients in the Statesboro area since 2001.

Dr. Kicklighter has been married to his wife Anna since 2002 and they have four boys. Dr. Kicklighter has been an active member of Pittman Park United Methodist Church since 1981. He strives to live out his faith daily.
